AAA Contrast Reference

Navy on White 9.5 : 1 · AAA Pass

Gold accent on Beige Decorative only · no text

Color Rules

  • Navy (#354566) for ALL text — headings, body, nav, buttons
  • Beige + White alternate as section backgrounds
  • Gold for underlines, labels, and hover only
  • Cream for cards sitting on white sections
  • Sand for borders and dividers

Never

  • Navy as a background fill (removed from nav/footer)
  • Gold as text color or large fill
  • Pure black (#000) anywhere
  • Cold blue-white combinations
  • More than 3 colors per section

  • Over-retouched skin
  • Cold clinical environments

09

Do's & Don'ts

The non-negotiable rules that protect the brand's identity.

Always

  • Navy (#354566) for all text
  • Beige + White alternating backgrounds
  • Poppins Light, Regular, or Medium only
  • Gold underline accents for emphasis
  • Generous whitespace between sections
  • Cosmetologist CTA on every page
  • Benefit-first, then rational proof
  • Real results from real clients

Never

  • Bold or italic in any context
  • Navy as a background color
  • Gold as text or large fill
  • Aggressive sales language
  • Cold, clinical color schemes
  • Generic stock photography
  • Body text smaller than 16px
  • Countdown timers or urgency tactics
